Math Car Race is a mathematical game that simulates a car race.
The car movement on the tracks are based on vectors and each move is determined by the previous vector.
The previous vector prolonged and the surrounding neighbor grid points can be selected.
Be careful not to go too fast, i.e. choose too long vectors or otherwise you will not stay on the track.

Features:
- Race on predefined tracks
- Generate a random track
- Create own tracks
- Let the computer drive a race track
